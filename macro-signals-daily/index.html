How Cross-Check Works

The system now uses FinBERT to classify financial sentiment in macro-related coverage. Market proxies are shown as context, but they are not treated as confirmation that inflation, growth, policy, liquidity, or risk moved in a specific direction.

  • FinBERT positive: positive financial sentiment.
  • FinBERT negative: negative financial sentiment.
  • FinBERT neutral: neutral financial sentiment.
  • Theme keywords identify the macro topics mentioned in each document.
